A little reminder

Nothing is better than a actual general diagnosis from a doctor! This app's database is incomplete, and bases off of the symptoms you have provided. You may accidentally input wrong symptoms, or you may have an illness not registered in the database. We remind you that this app should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you have any doubts or concerns, you should consult a qualified healthcare provider before using any medications or treatments. For all the given diseases, if they are not critical, it is recommended to possibly go to a health facility that may be able to confirm that you do indeed have the disease before trying any aid or medicine. Finally, we recommend that you use this app as a tool to complement your healthcare provider's advice and not as a replacement. Please seek professional medical advice if you are experiencing severe symptoms or have any doubts or concerns about your health.
